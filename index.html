About Sunrisers Hyderabad

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) is a Hyderabad-based franchise cricket team representing the city in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Established in 2012, SRH has quickly become known for its strong bowling attack, incredible fan base, and passionate commitment to the game. With a consistent performance record, SRH lifted the IPL trophy in 2016 and continues to strive for excellence every season.
